What are narrative surveys? Essentially they are interactive stories that a reader can actively navigate. Any navigation action is recorded in a dataset and allows for later analysis of choice-making, understanding, and persepctive-taking. Narrative Surveys also support text entry, which can be used to record participants' thoughts and intentions.

Surveys

Now, how to get started? Click "create a survey" below and then "edit" to enter the authoring tool. After the authoring is done and you have tested the survey, you can send it to participants and let them go through the survey on their own. The data analysis view for every survey is accessible from the survey (click on "data analysis").
