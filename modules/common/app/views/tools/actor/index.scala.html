Scripting

What are scripts? Scripts are small piece of Javascript that are triggered by events on an OOCSI channel. After being triggered they can do a lot of different things in reaction: update an IoT dataset or diary dataset, retrieve and update a record in an entity dataset, or even send a message to a participant on Telegram.

The important point is that these scripts can do logic, filtering, and more for you, and it's all running in the Data Foundry. Find more infomration on scripting in the documentation.
